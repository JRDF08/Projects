SoundScape (Spotify-Inspired App)

Description:

SoundScape is a personalized music discovery app built using the Spotify API, designed to enhance your listening experience. Connect your Spotify account to access millions of songs, albums, and playlists while enjoying a unique interface with features. With SoundScape, you can explore your favorite tracks in new ways, discover hidden gems, and enjoy a more curated and interactive experience.

How to use:

Create an Account or Login

  • Click start button located at the bottom part of the app and sign up for a new account using your email address, phone number, Facebook, Apple, or Google account.
  • If you already have an account, simply login with your credentials.

    • Button

  • You can click the icon or the name of the app "SoundScape" located at the left top of the app to go back to the Top Playlist.
  • Use the Search Button located at top middle of the app to find specific songs, artists, albums or podcasts.
  • You can click "About Button" located at the top right of the app in order to go back on this "About-App Section".

    • Explore Music and Podcasts

  • Upon login you will redirect to Top Playlist recommended by Spotify API.
  • Explore curated playlists, genres, moods, or charts to discover new music.
